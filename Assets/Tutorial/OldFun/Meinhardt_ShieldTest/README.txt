Shader Test Scene Controls (in Play Mode only):

Left Mouse Click - "Shoot Shield" with Raycast
Space - Activate / Deactivate Shield


On the object Shield > Shield Manager, select the from the three activation types:
-Linear: shield activates along the normalized direction vector
-Radial Outwards: shield activates from activation origin outwards
-Radial Inwards: shield activates inwards and ends at activation origin
